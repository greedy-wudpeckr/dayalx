## FULL PROCESS BREAKDOWN (Detailed Steps)

### Phase 1 — Onboarding
- **Section badge:** `Phase 01`
- **Headline:** `"We Start by Understanding Your Lab"`
- **Layout:** Left text, right illustration
- **Sub-steps (numbered list):**
  1. You fill a simple onboarding form (subjects, batch size, university, semester dates)
  2. Our team reviews your syllabus and practical session list
  3. We send you a proposed Kit Specification Document for approval
  4. You review, request changes, and sign off
- **Visual:** Mockup of onboarding form / kit spec document
- **Timeline indicator:** Step 1 of 3, animated teal progress bar

### Phase 2 — Kit Building
- **Section badge:** `Phase 02`
- **Headline:** `"We Build Your Kit — Item by Item"`
- **Layout:** Right text, left illustration
- **Sub-steps:**
  1. Our procurement team sources all approved items
  2. Every item is quality-checked against the specification
  3. Items are pre-portioned, labelled, and organized by experiment
  4. Kit is packed, sealed, and given a unique Kit ID
  5. Final packing list is generated and attached inside the kit
- **Visual:** Illustration of kit assembly line / packing process

### Phase 3 — Delivery & Support
- **Section badge:** `Phase 03`
- **Headline:** `"Your Kit Arrives Before Your Semester Begins"`
- **Layout:** Left text, right illustration
- **Sub-steps:**
  1. Kit is dispatched with 7–10 days buffer before semester start
  2. Real-time delivery tracking via your dashboard
  3. Lab coordinator receives packing list + item guide via email
  4. DAYALX support available for any item queries or replacements
  5. Reorder reminder sent 4 weeks before next semester
- **Visual:** Delivery tracking dashboard mockup
